About the Simulator

This simulator shows how the Hitchhiker's Guide content appears on the LilyGo T-Deck device. The display area represents the actual screen size and resolution of the T-Deck, allowing you to preview how the guide's text, graphics, and interface elements will look when integrated into the physical book.

The content is fetched from the project page and formatted to match the T-Deck's display characteristics, including monospace font rendering and limited screen space.
